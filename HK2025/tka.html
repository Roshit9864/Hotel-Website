About Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular vegetarian Indian appetizer made from cubes of paneer marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled or baked until slightly charred. It's often served on skewers with onions and bell peppers, alongside mint chutney.

Key Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Thick yogurt, lemon juice
  • Ginger, garlic paste
  • Spices: chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  • Bell peppers, onions (for skewers)

Fun Facts

  • Paneer Tikka is often used as a pizza topping in fusion dishes.
  • Can be cooked on a grill, in an oven, or even in an air fryer.
  • It's a top vegetarian BBQ choice in India and abroad.
