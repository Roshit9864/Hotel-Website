Pork Stick: Ugandan Street BBQ

Delicious grilled pork skewers — Uganda’s favorite roadside indulgence.

What is a Pork Stick?

Known locally as “Muchomo”, pork sticks are juicy cubes of spiced pork grilled over open flames. Found in roadside stalls and bars across Uganda, they're popular for their bold flavor and street food charm.

Key Ingredients

  • Pork (with some fat): Cut into chunks for tenderness.
  • Garlic, Ginger, Chili: Spiced marinades for flavor.
  • Paprika, Salt, Lemon Juice: To enhance smokiness.
  • Skewers: Bamboo or metal sticks for grilling.

How It’s Served

Usually paired with a spicy dipping sauce and roasted bananas or cassava. Often eaten while socializing with friends or family — best served hot off the grill.

Fun Facts

  • "Muchomo" literally means "roasted meat" in Luganda.
  • Pork joints are famous social spots in Ugandan neighborhoods.
  • Some joints marinate their meat for over 24 hours for that juicy bite!
